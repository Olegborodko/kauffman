Kauffman Fellow Report – Journal

The Kauffman Fellows Report is an annual, journal-style compilation of executive reports authored by members of the Kauffman Fellows Network, examining all aspects of the capital formation process. The Kauffman Fellows Report began in 2010, and brings rigorous attention to the discussion of venture, capital formation, and innovation worldwide.

The Kauffman Fellows Report compiles and shares the latest findings on capital formation and innovation from across the global network. The focus is on applied, industry solutions that foster the vital process of new business creation. Contributors examine trends with a pragmatic eye and propose original, practical approaches that can be adopted and embraced in the years and decades to come.

We invite each member of the community to engage with us in this critical evaluation of real-world challenges and opportunities in front of us all, and look forward to the conversations that these articles will trigger.

Each volume of the Kauffman Fellows Report is available here, released a few months after the hard copy printing. The journal is also available for redistribution. If you’d like to make a volume available to your community of practice please complete the hard copy request form.

Kauffman Fellow Books

We produce 1-2 books each year from the Kauffman Fellows community. The Kauffman Fellows Program is structured as a laboratory and each class produces original field projects, ranging from venture funds in the West Bank of the Palestinian Territories to technology incubators in Nigeria to impact funds in the United States; these projects often inspire book ideas.

To use content from the Kauffman Fellows Report or from a Kauffman Fellows Press book, please submit a formal request for reprint permission.
